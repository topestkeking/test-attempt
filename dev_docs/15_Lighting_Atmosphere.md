# Role: Lighting & Atmosphere Lead

## Overview
The sun, the dust, and the shadows. Essential for the "Grimy" feel.

## UE 5.8 Specifics
- **Lumen:** Real-time global illumination for metallic reflections.
- **Volumetric Clouds:** 5.8's improved atmosphere for the "Desert Heat" look.

## Key Tasks
1. Configure the "Day/Night" cycle.
2. Setup "Lens Flares" that look gritty and industrial.
3. Optimize shadows for fast-moving vehicles.

## AI Prompt Examples
- *"Adjust the sun angle to 4:00 PM and increase the volumetric fog density."*
- *"Add a warm orange tint to the shadows to simulate a sunset in a dusty wasteland."*

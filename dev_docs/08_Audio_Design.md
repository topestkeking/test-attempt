# Role: Audio Designer (Mechanical & Industrial)

## Overview
The roar of engines, the clank of metal, and the whistle of projectiles.

## UE 5.8 Specifics
- **MetaSounds:** Use MetaSounds for procedural engine noises that change based on RPM and Mass.
- **Audio Modulation:** Dynamically change sound pitch based on the "Scale" of the part.

## Key Tasks
1. Setup dynamic engine sound loops.
2. Create "Impact" sounds for different material types (Sheet Metal vs. Heavy Steel).
3. Implement 3D spatialization for competitive sound cues (hearing an enemy sneak up).

## AI Prompt Examples
- *"Create a MetaSound graph that increases the bass of the engine as the vehicle's mass increases."*
- *"Add a clanking metal sound effect whenever the vehicle lands from a jump."*
